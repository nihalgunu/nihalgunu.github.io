Phyvant

2025

Every company has decades of knowledge trapped in its people's heads. How things actually work, why exceptions exist, what the documentation never captured. When those people leave, the knowledge leaves with them. When AI agents try to do their jobs, they fail — because nobody ever wrote it down.

Phyvant builds the brain of your organization. We watch how your business actually operates — how your best people make decisions, resolve edge cases, correct mistakes — and turn that into a living knowledge system that every AI agent in the company can tap into. The goal is simple: make every AI agent as smart as everyone in the company combined.

No consultants. No 6-month documentation project. Phyvant learns from your team's real work and gets smarter every day.
