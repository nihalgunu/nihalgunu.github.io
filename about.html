About

I build AI systems for places where you can't afford to be wrong. That means enterprise AI at Phyvant and robotics research at Purdue.

I like building infrastructure that works under constraints. Private, accurate, and fast — when conventional wisdom says pick two.

Long-term, I want to build category-defining vertical models that outperform general-purpose AI where precision, trust, and domain context are non-negotiable.

Technical contributions

  • Founder of Phyvant — specialized enterprise AI with verified organizational context.
  • Helped build RLHF and model quality systems at Mercor.
  • First author, PathCluster: Pedestrian Group-Adaptive Social Navigation in Dense Crowds — IROS 2025 (with Aniket Bera).
  • MIRACLE: Inverse Reinforcement and Curriculum Learning Model for Human-inspired Mobile Robot Navigation — arXiv 2023 (with Kshitij Tiwari and Aniket Bera).
